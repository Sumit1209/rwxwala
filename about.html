About Our Institution
RWX is the new destination for industry-based IT training. Our business philosophy is solely based on human values, quality education, and personal relationships. Our core concepts like “value for money, need analysis, agreeable customers, and preparing them with an improved industrial orientation” are imprinted on top of our minds.
Unlike countless other IT training institutes, New IIT is not just a money-making organization. Here we place high emphasis on value thanks to our highly qualified training personnel with profound bases in some of the country's reputed IT companies. Here we don’t just possess the latest technologies, software, and gadgets but also the most experienced and skilled hands to offer the most customer-friendly personalized solutions.
